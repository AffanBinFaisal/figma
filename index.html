The Flower Station Family of Companies

Flower Station

Flower Station's journey started towards the end of 2001 with what still remains its flagship flower shop. It was set up in place of, what was once, a petrol station perfectly situated on london's Park Road, North London's gates to Central London.

We are London's first ever drive-through florist, which is opne 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and is easily accessible to all.

As well as several shops, a garden centre and cafes, we are also proud to be a leading online florist. We are regarded across London and the rest of the United Kingdom as the number one florist. This is an accomplishment we are proud to hold, yet one that we are always striving to maintain.

In the early years, Flower Station was a little florist barely serving customers within a mile radius and struggling to survive yet there was great potential. Directors had a vision and decided to persist and invest every resource into their plan for the business. This dedication to customer service and passion for the business had led Flower Station from being just your local flower shop to a recognised London brand.

Chance is these days you will most likely come across one or many of its delivery vans buzziing around London on daily basis. Yes, today, Flower Station is proud to be one of the leading florists in London and Uk with several shops scattered across London as well as having a sister company equally as large

Our aim was and still is, to bring great florist work at affordable prices

Our bouquets are made to order by expert florists, from all over the world, and flowers are our passion. Through this journey, we have embraced social media channels and we like to share our news and tips as well as show that there are real people working behind the website and that we do care.
